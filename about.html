About — Body & Glow

Velvet Charms — Body & Glow creates gentle, handmade bodycare and cozy knitted pieces designed to feel like a warm hug. Our soaps, creams and natural perfumes are made in small batches from nourishing ingredients (shea butter, almond oil, botanicals) — ideal for sensitive skin and perfect holiday stocking stuffers.

We also offer personalized gift packaging and curated bundles for Christmas: choose scents, colors and size options and we'll prepare a beautiful hand-wrapped present ready to gift. Fast custom requests? Send us the details and we’ll recommend the perfect combination.
