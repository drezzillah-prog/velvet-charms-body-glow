Handmade gifts, personal touches

Velvet Charms crafts small-batch soaps, creams, perfumes and candles meant to be unwrapped and remembered. Every piece is made with gentle ingredients and a focus on texture and scent that feels like a warm memory.

Customisation & commissions

Looking for the perfect, personal gift? We specialise in customised holiday sets and commissioned perfumes and soaps. When ordering a custom piece you can:

  • Choose materials, colors and scents
  • Send a reference photo or short description
  • Request initials, a short message, or a color palette

How to order custom work: Use the customization box on the product page to add notes and mention your preferences. You may upload one supporting file (image or PDF) via the contact form; if you need to share more, message us on Instagram and we’ll provide a secure upload link. We confirm a mockup before starting to make sure you love the result.

Why shop handmade?

Each order supports a small studio and helps preserve traditional craft skills. Your purchase becomes a keepsake — and a story we are proud to make.
