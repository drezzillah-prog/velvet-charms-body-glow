    About Velvet Charms — Body Glow

    Velvet Charms began as a tiny studio where warmth, craft and care met. We make small-batch candles, handcrafted soaps and natural bodycare designed to make everyday rituals feel special. Every item is hand-finished with attention to texture and scent, and we personalise commissions for meaningful gifts.

    Why choose our pieces?

    • Handmade with care: We craft each piece in small batches to preserve quality and character.
    • Personalised options: Add a note when purchasing if you'd like a specific scent, colour or small custom detail.
    • Ethical ingredients: Natural oils, responsibly-sourced waxes and gentle skincare formulations.
    • Thoughtful packing: We wrap each order with a seasonal touch — perfect for gifting.

    How to order a custom commission

    On each product's page you can add a short customisation note (colours, preferred scent, or reference). After you press Buy with PayPal, paste that note into PayPal's 'note to seller' area so we receive it with your order. If you'd like help before ordering, message us on Instagram or email — we'll walk you through sizes, timelines and any special requests.
