Welcome to Velvet Charms — handmade with care

At Velvet Charms we make cozy, heart-warming handmade treasures — candles, soaps, knitted winterwear, paintings, epoxy home pieces and small baubles designed to become little keepsakes in your life.

Our approach

Everything you see is made with attention to detail and a love for texture: slow-crafted candles with sculptural reliefs, soaps hand-cut and fragranced with essential oils, knitted winter sets brushed for ultimate softness, and mixed-media artworks that combine paint and clay for depth and personality. We source gentle materials and prioritize safe, skin-friendly formulations for our creams and soaps.
